About: This is a timer that can be used to track the discussion and nomination phases for the social deduction game Blood on the Clocktower. The timer controls can be found at the bottom, allowing to you start, stop, reset, edit, and quick adjust the current time. You can mute all sounds with the "sound" icon. To recall the town immediately (stopping the timer) you can press the bell icon. To switch between dawn, dusk and night (discussion & nomination phases), press the sun/moon/eye icon. You can move to the next day by pressing the calendar icon. Switching to dusk (nomination phase) will automatically shorten the timer. The timer will also be shortened when advancing the days (to account for fewer alive people as the game progresses). Hope you enjoy!

You may also control this app using keyboard-shortcuts:

Space
Start or stop the timer
Backspace
Reset the timer
Z
Edit the timer
Up/Right/+,
Down/Left/-
Quick adjust the timer
R
Immediate recall town
N/Enter
Move to next phase (night/dawn/dusk/next night)
F1, F2, F3
Goto current day's dawn/dusk/night
F4
Goto next day's dawn
D
Toggle Mute
F
Toggle Fullscreen
Q
Toggle this dialogue
