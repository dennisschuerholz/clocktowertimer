Tips for Use: Dawn is a longer timer, and can be used when people are discussing during the day. Dusk is a shorter timer, and can be used when it is time for deliberation and nominations. You can toggle between the two by clicking Dawn/Dusk. Clicking Proceed to the next day will make the timer shorter, as there are less people in play. You can increment the timer by 15 seconds using the arrows, or enter a custom time by clicking the keyboard icon. Hope you enjoy!
